README: AIRMAIL - PAR AVION

Airmail - Par Avion uses Cufon and Inkburrow for the navigational items. Inkburrow is a free font by Udi-Udi and can be found in the  airmail-parAvion/Resources along with the PSD files for the RSS Icon and Airmail Sticker.
